WHAT IS CYBER LAWS?

Cyber laws is a term to describe the legal issues realted to use of communication technology, particularly "cyberspace" i.e. internet. Cyber laws supervise the digital circulation of information, software, information security, e-commerce, and monetary transactions.

"CYBER LAWS ARE FORMED TO PUNISH PEOPLE WHO PERFORM ANY ILLEGAL ACTIVITY ONLINE."




Cyber Laws in INDIA

In simple way we can say that cyber crime is unlawful acts where in the computer is either a tool a target or both.
we can categorise cyber crime in two ways:

1. COMPUTER AS ATARGET:
Using a computer to attack other computer.
for ex:- Hacking, virus/DoS attacks, etc.
2. COMPUTER AS A WEAPON:
Using a computer to commit real world crimes.
for ex:- Cyber Terrorism, EFT Frauds, Bank Frauds, ATM Frauds, etc.
